NovaAdapt Operator turns your Android phone into the remote cockpit for NovaAdapt.

Pair your phone in one scan:
- scan a pairing QR from the desktop console
- open a NovaAdapt deep link
- paste a pairing code or manifest

Once paired, you can:
- approve or reject high-impact actions
- watch jobs and plans in real time
- monitor desktop, browser, mobile, and IoT control flows
- access the live terminal console remotely
- stay connected to your NovaAdapt runtime without typing bridge URLs manually

Built for operators, solo builders, and vibe coders who want the power of NovaAdapt without manual setup friction.
