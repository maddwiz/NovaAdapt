NovaAdapt Operator Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

NovaAdapt Operator is a remote control surface for a NovaAdapt runtime that you configure. The Android app does not require a NovaAdapt-operated account and is designed to connect to infrastructure you own or control.

What The App Stores

  • Bridge URL and websocket URL
  • Pairing payloads and imported operator tokens
  • Device identifier and local auto-connect preferences

Where Data Goes

The app sends commands, approvals, and runtime requests only to the NovaAdapt bridge/runtime configured by the user. If that runtime is connected to cloud model providers or other services, those downstream data flows are controlled by the operator's NovaAdapt backend configuration.

What The App Does Not Require

  • No NovaAdapt cloud account is required
  • No always-on vendor account is required
  • No mandatory analytics account is required

Camera Use

The app may request camera access only when the user chooses to scan a NovaAdapt pairing QR code. Camera access is not required for manual pairing via links, shared text, or pasted pairing codes.

Security Responsibility

NovaAdapt Operator reflects the security posture of the bridge and runtime you connect it to. Operators are responsible for protecting bridge tokens, pairing manifests, device allowlists, and any third-party model-provider credentials configured in their NovaAdapt deployment.
